My philosophy

Artificial intelligence is a field that affects many different sectors and is becoming increasingly integrated into our everyday work and life. Such a wide-ranging technology cannot be designed solely by experts in applied technological sciences, but must also take into account the opinions of artists, humanists and social scientists. Creating an ethical technology that takes into account a wide range of opinions is essential for the development of generative artificial intelligence (GAI) and multimodal models.
